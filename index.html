Hollow Wood – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 November 2025

Hollow Wood is a Twitch Extension that allows viewers to play a mini-game, earn levels and skins, and compete on channel and global leaderboards. This Privacy Policy explains what information we receive, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Receive

Identity Information

When you use Hollow Wood on Twitch, the extension may receive:

  • Twitch User ID – only if you choose to share your identity with the extension
  • Twitch Opaque ID – if you do not share identity (this does not reveal who you are)

Gameplay & Usage Data

  • The Channel ID where the extension is running
  • Your game scores and your best score per channel
  • Your weekly free-play usage (how many free plays you have used this week)
  • Progression data such as level, XP, prestige, total games, and cosmetic skin selections
  • Bits transaction IDs processed by the extension (for example, for paid plays)

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Email address
  • Real name
  • Payment information
  • Twitch password
  • IP address
  • Chat messages
  • Any personal information not explicitly listed above

2. How We Use This Information

Your information is used only to operate the Hollow Wood extension, including to:

  • Display your scores on channel and global leaderboards
  • Track and enforce weekly free-play limits
  • Manage progression, such as XP, level, prestige, and cosmetic unlocks
  • Process Bits-based plays and prevent duplicate use of the same transaction
  • Provide testing/VIP features for broadcasters (when enabled)
  • Improve or maintain extension functionality

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Hollow Wood stores and processes data using the following trusted services:

  • Twitch API services (for identity, display names, and extension authentication)
  • Supabase (database and secure storage)
  • Hosting provider used for the extension’s backend (for example, Render or a similar service)

These services store data securely and only as needed for the operation of the extension. We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Gameplay and progression data may be stored for as long as the Hollow Wood extension is active.

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7). Upon request, your data will be permanently removed from our database within 30 days.

5. Security

All communication between the extension and our backend uses HTTPS encryption. Stored data is protected using Supabase’s database security controls and industry-standard safeguards.

Only minimal data required for gameplay is collected. While no online system is 100% secure, reasonable measures are taken to protect data against loss, misuse, or unauthorized access.

6. Children’s Privacy

Hollow Wood is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years old.

If you believe data has been collected from someone under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Choices & Rights

Identity Sharing

You may choose not to share your Twitch identity with the extension. In that case, Hollow Wood uses a non-identifying opaque ID, and certain features (such as named leaderboards or skin progression) may be limited.

Stopping Use

You can stop using the extension at any time by closing or disabling it on Twitch.

Requesting Data Removal or Access

You may request a copy of the data stored about you, or request deletion of your data, by contacting the developer directly (see Section 8).
